About Me

Future physician passionate about machine learning, data, and making an impact.

Hi there! I'm Ian, currently a 3rd-year medical student at the Warren Alpert Medical School of Brown University in Providence, Rhode Island.

My passion lies at the intersection of machine learning, data science, and healthcare. I believe that we can leverage modern advances in machine learning in concert with vast amounts of biomedical data to ultimately provide faster, safer, and better care for patients.

In my free time, I enjoy binge-watching the latest TV shows, lifting heavy things, chilling out with music, and eating ice cream.

I was born in Illinois but consider myself a native of Los Angeles, California who still hasn't acclimated to New England winters.
